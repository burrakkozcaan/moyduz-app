# Refund Policy

Last updated: February 2025

## Introduction

At Moyduz, we strive to provide exceptional services. This Refund Policy outlines the terms under which refunds may be granted.

## Refund Eligibility

- **Service Cancellation:** Full refund if cancelled within 48 hours of purchase and before work has commenced
- **Non-Delivery:** If we fail to deliver within the agreed timeframe
- **Technical Issues:** Significant problems we cannot resolve within 30 days
- **Duplicate Charges or Billing Errors**

## Non-Refundable

- Completed and delivered services
- Custom development work already started
- Digital products that have been accessed
- Subscription fees for elapsed periods

## Refund Process

1. Email [info@moyduz.com](mailto:info@moyduz.com) with subject "Refund Request"
2. Include order number, date of purchase, and reason
3. We review within 5-7 business days
4. If approved, refund processed within 10-14 business days
