# Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

## Introduction

At Moyduz, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our services or visit our website.

## Information We Collect

**Personal Information:** Name, email, phone, business details, payment information, account credentials.

**Automatically Collected:** IP address, browser type, device information, usage data, cookies.

## How We Use Your Information

- To provide, maintain, and improve our services
- To process transactions and manage your account
- To communicate with you about updates
- To personalize your experience
- To ensure security and prevent fraud
- To comply with legal obligations

## Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only with service providers, when required by law, or with your consent.

## Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or transfer your data. Contact us at [privacy@moyduz.com](mailto:privacy@moyduz.com) to exercise these rights.
